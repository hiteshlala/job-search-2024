Senior Software Engineer, Full Stack - Capital One Software (Remote)
Capital One Software is seeking a  Senior Software Engineer  who is passionate about marrying innovation with emerging technologies. 

We are a new enterprise B2B software business focused on providing cloud and data management solutions to companies operating in the cloud.  This is Capital One’s first foray into the enterprise software market and comes after years spent building our own in-house cloud and data management tools that enable us to operate at scale in the cloud. As a Capital One Software Engineer, you’ll have the opportunity to build meaningful software that enables our customers to manage their data securely and efficiently at any scale.  Additionally as a Capital One Engineer you get to collaborate with some of the best in this business. 

What You’ll Do:

Write high quality code, perform code reviews

Collaborate across agile teams to deliver robust cloud-based solutions that drive powerful experiences for our customers

Participate in design discussions

Research and propose innovative features to product managers

Basic Qualifications: 

Bachelor’s Degree

At least 3 years of experience in software engineering (Internship experience does not apply)

Preferred Qualifications:

Master's Degree

4+ years of experience in at least one of the following: JavaScript, Java, TypeScript, SQL, Python, or GoLang

1+ years of experience with AWS, GCP, Microsoft Azure, or another cloud service

2+ years of experience in Agile practices.

1+ years of experience with software development in Rust, C or C++
